How It Works

1

Join Our Discord Server

The first step is to join our Discord community. This is where all test coordination happens and where you'll be able to request a test, communicate with testers, and receive your results.

Not in our Discord yet? Join using the button at the top of the page or click the button below to get started!

4

Wait for a Tester

After submitting your request, a tester will contact you through the ticket to coordinate the test. They will let you know when they're available and set up a time for your test.

5

Complete Your Test

Join the game at the scheduled time and complete your test in your chosen gamemode. The tester will evaluate your performance based on our tier rating system.

6

Receive Your Results

After the test is complete, the tester will provide your tier ranking and feedback through your ticket. You'll know exactly where you stand in your chosen gamemode!
